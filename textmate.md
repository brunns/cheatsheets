# Textmate

## General

|**Command**|**Function**|
|-|-|
|⌘t|Quick open|
|⌘r|Run file|

## Search and replace

|**Command**|**Function**|
|-|-|
|⇧⌘f|Find in project|
|⌘f|Find|
|⌘e|Use selection as find|

## Editing

|**Command**|**Function**|
|-|-|
|F5|Sort lines|
|⌃⇧d|Duplicate line|
|⌃⌘ (↑↓)|Move line|
|⌘]|Indent|
|⌘[|Dedent|
